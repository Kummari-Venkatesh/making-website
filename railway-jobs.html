Railway Jobs (RRB - Group D)

Job Details

  • Position: Group D, Track Maintainer, Helper, Pointsman, Porter
  • Salary: ₹18,000 - ₹22,000 per month + allowances
  • Age Criteria: 18 - 33 years

Exam Pattern

  • Stage 1: Computer-Based Test (CBT) – General Awareness, Arithmetic, Reasoning
  • Stage 2: Physical Efficiency Test (PET)
  • Stage 3: Document Verification

Study Resources

  • Books: RRB Group D by RPH Editorial Board, General Knowledge by Lucent
  • Websites: Indiabix, Testbook, Gradeup
  • YouTube Channels: Unacademy Railway Exams, Adda247
